## Abstract

This AGU Fall Meeting 2024 iPoster uses new Koh-i-Sultan ages, whole-rock
major and trace elements, and Sr-Nd-Pb isotopes, together with published Taftan
and Bazman datasets, to test how thick Indus Fan sediments are recycled into
the Makran volcanic arc.

The poster highlights regional sediment-mantle mixing trends and discusses why
individual volcanic centers still preserve different isotope and trace-element
signals during mantle melting, magma differentiation, and ascent.

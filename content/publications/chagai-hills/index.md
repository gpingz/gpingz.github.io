## Abstract

This study investigates subduction- to collision-related magmatism in the
Chagai Hills to clarify how the India-Eurasia collision system evolved through
time. The work combines geochronological, petrological, and geochemical
evidence to evaluate changing magma sources, crustal contributions, and
tectonic setting.

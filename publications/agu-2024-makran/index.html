Abstract

This AGU Fall Meeting 2024 presentation reports whole-rock elemental and Sr-Nd-Pb isotope constraints on sediment recycling at the Makran arc. The work connects geochemical systematics with sediment inputs and arc volcanic outputs.

Dr. Guangping Zeng
Postdoctoral Researcher in Marine Geology
I am a marine geologist interested in Earth material cycles across environments, resources, oceans, crust, and mantle. My work connects sedimentary records, isotope geochemistry, petrology, ocean drilling, and data synthesis to understand environmental change, resource formation, and deep recycling of carbon, water, and other key materials.

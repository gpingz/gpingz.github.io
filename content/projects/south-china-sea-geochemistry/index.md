This ongoing exploratory project focuses on fresh basalt samples from spreading
ridge settings in the South China Sea.

The goal is to combine radiogenic isotopes and stable isotopes to trace deep
material cycling, mantle-source variability, and ocean-basin evolution in a
marginal sea setting. This work extends my research from sedimentary basins and
subduction systems into ocean drilling and oceanic lithosphere questions.
